Booking Policy

    Cancellation and Refunds

  • Cancellations or changes to reservations can be made up to 24 hours before the scheduled visit.

  • Refunds will not be issued for cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled visit


    • Children and Age Restrictions

  • Children under a certain 2 years old may enter the musuem for free or when accompanied by a paying adult.

  • Age restrictions may apply to certain exhibits or events; please check individual exhibit guidelines.


    • Photography and Filming

  • Photography and filming is restricted in certain areas within the museum.

  • If you do not comply with the restrictions, we have the right to terminate your ticket and kick you out without a refund.


    • Security Checks

  • All visitors may be subject to security checks upon entry. Large bags, backpacks, or prohibited items may not be allowed inside the museum.


    • Code of Conduct

  • Disruptive behavior, vandalism, or any form of disrespect towards staff will not be tolerated.

  • Visitors must also comply to the cultural dress code. Any inappropriate clothing isn't allowed inside the museum.
