Frequently Asked Questions

    Where is The National Museum of Egyptian Civilization located?

  • It is located in Ein as Seirah, Old Cairo, Cairo


    • What are the operating hours of the museum?

  • Saturday – Thursday from 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM
    Friday from 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM


    • Is there a gift shop?

  • Yes, we have a gift shop where you can find unique souvenirs, books, and gifts related to the museum's exhibits and themes.


    • Is there a café or restaurant on-site?

  • Yes, we have a cafe where you can relax and enjoy refreshments.


    • Is the museuem wheelchair accessible?

  • Yes, we strive to make our museum accessible to all visitors.
