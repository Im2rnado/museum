Coffin of King Ramses II

The Coffin of King Ramses II, a regal masterpiece from 13th century BCE Egypt, adorned with intricate hieroglyphics and gold leaf, encapsulates the grandeur of Ramses the Great's reign and offers insights into ancient burial practices and cultural traditions.






Royal mummy of Hatshepsut

The Royal Mummy of Hatshepsut, an ancient Egyptian queen and pharaoh, represents a fascinating archaeological discovery showcasing the preserved remains of this influential female ruler.
